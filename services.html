Common Questions and Answers

Do you offer services that are not listed on your website?

Yes! We offer any service that you wish to have done and if we don't, we can find someone who does offer what you need.

What if I am not sure about a project and the costs of it?

We offer free estimates and walk through the entire project and costs with you before we start so you will know the cost, estimated time, and what the finished product will look like.

How long does it take before my project will begin?

That depends on how busy we are and what projects we have been working on but usually once your get your estimate and plans, we will begin within the next month.

What if something goes wrong during construction?

No need to worry! We are licensed and insured so if something does go wrong everyone will be covered.
